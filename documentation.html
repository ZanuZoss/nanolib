IPSW flashing is the process of installing or restoring firmware on an Apple device using an IPSW file. This is commonly used to update, downgrade, or return a device to factory settings.

  1. Download the correct IPSW file for your device model.
  2. Put the device into DFU or Recovery Mode.
  3. Use iTunes or a third-party tool to select and flash the IPSW file.
  4. Wait for the flashing to complete. Do not disconnect the device during this step.
  5. Once finished, your device should reboot with the new firmware installed.
